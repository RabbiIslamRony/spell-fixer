How to use

1

Type in any text box

Spell Fixer activates automatically on every text field across any website.

2

Misspelled words get a red underline

Click the underline to see suggestions for the correct spelling.

3

Click a suggestion or add to your dictionary

Your custom words are saved permanently and never flagged again.

Good to know

Words in ALL CAPS, URLs, and numbers are never flagged.
Click the extension icon to turn spell checking on or off globally.
You can disable Spell Fixer on specific sites using the popup.
Your custom dictionary is saved locally — no data is sent anywhere.
