Spell Fixer

Privacy Policy

Version 1.0.0  ·  Effective date: April 2026

Spell Fixer is a browser extension that checks your spelling in real time as you type in text boxes on any website. This page explains exactly what data the extension does and does not use.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not read, record, or transmit the text you type.
  • We do not track which websites you visit.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting.
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Data Stored Locally

The only data Spell Fixer stores is your personal custom word list — words you choose to add to your dictionary via the "Add to dictionary" button. This list is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your own device. It never leaves your browser.

  • Storage location: your local browser only.
  • You can clear it at any time by removing the extension.
  • No backups are made anywhere else.

Network Requests

Spell Fixer makes zero network requests. All spell checking is performed entirely offline using a built-in dictionary bundled with the extension. No text is ever sent to any server.

Permissions

The extension requests the storage permission solely to save your custom word list locally. No other permissions are requested.

Third-Party Services

No third-party services, SDKs, or APIs are used.
