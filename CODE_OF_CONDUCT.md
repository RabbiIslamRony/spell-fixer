# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming project, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in Spell Fixer a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other contributors

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Toxic or abusive language
- Personal attacks, insults, or shaming
- Posting illegal content or hateful material
- Targeting people because of protected characteristics

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please file an issue or contact the repository owner directly through GitHub.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or ban participants at their discretion if behavior does not comply with this code of conduct.
